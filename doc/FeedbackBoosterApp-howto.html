Feedback Booster App

1. Equipments

  1. Vibration Speaker (Exciter) with amp
  2. Small guitar amp
  3. Microphone
  4. Audio Interface
    PC built-in Mic and headphone out can be used but not recommended because mic should be close to the guitar amp.

2. Connection

Location of vibration speaker: head of the neck or body 

Guitar ----> Guitar Amp ----> Mic or amp line/headphone output 
vib. SP                         |
   ^                            | input
   |                            V
   +--------------- Audio Interface (PC)
       output

3. Controls

A. unprocessed feedback

  1. Choose input (audio) port
  2. Choose output (audio) port -- audio I/O starts
  3. Adjust volume to have feedback

B. Manually Equalized feedback

Choose input and output(audio) port as in A.
  1. press "bypass" button
  2. Adjust peakHz, eQGain, and Q
  3. Adjust OctMix (-1 is for fundamental freq only, 1 is one octave up only)
  4. Adjust Comp
    Default: Ratio 1 and Threshold 0 denotes no comp
    1. Adjust CompGain not to Clip(red)
    2. Turn up compRatio to 2 or more
    3. Turn down CompThreash to -20, 40dB (COMP(green) denotes comp is effective)
  5. Adjust volume

C. Automatically Equalized feedback

  1. Press "manualEQ" to switch to AutoEQ mode.
    Then peakHz becomes automatic,
    peakHz slider will be disabled,
    and detected pitch(freq) will be shown.
  2. The other controls are same as in B.
